Public projects

This is a collection of some of my fully public projects.
Some of these are old and many were made quickly between other commitments.
Hopefully they may be useful or intriguing to someone other than myself.
Consult each project itself for full license information, but most will be MIT.

  • Hardlist Leaderboard (Celeste)

    A leaderboard calculator based on the community Celeste Hardlist

  • Iterative Song Stitcher

    A collection of Python scripts to iterate through audio files, cutting between them at defined points. Originally created for "every song name plays that song" type creations.

  • Absurd Date Parser

    A quick project that can parse absurd dates (like 25.25-25.25-25.25) into mathematically equivalent real ones.
